Banner By Period
Description
The purpose of the banner by period is to transform a spreadsheet that presents a SKU banner from horizontal weeks by column to a database format, to present by banner, SKU, week.
Instructions
  1. Upload a file, e.g. QUEBEC_SKU_WEEK_PRICE.csv.
  2. The application here will work to generate a well-formatted result.
  3. When everything is ready, a download link will be created.
  4. Click on the download link to conclude the transformation.
